Building Management Systems (BMS)

Intro

Building Management Systems can improve building efficiency and sustainability by automatically adjusting system settings based on occupancy, weather conditions, and other factors. BMS can also provide building owners and managers with detailed performance data and analytics to help identify areas for improvement and optimize operations.

How does it work?

A Building Management System is composed of various hardware and software components that work together to gather data from the building's various systems and equipment, analyse the data, and automate or recommend adjustments to the systems based on that analysis. The system uses sensors, meters, and other measurement devices to collect data on energy usage, temperature, humidity, and other building parameters, which are then analysed to identify areas where improvements can be made to increase energy efficiency, reduce costs, and improve occupant comfort and safety.
