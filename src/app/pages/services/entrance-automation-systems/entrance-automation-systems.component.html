Entrance Automation Systems

Intro

At Entrance Automation Systems, we understand that security and ease of access are two crucial elements for any property owner. Our range of automated solutions provides you with both. Whether you're looking to secure your commercial premises, control the flow of people into a public space, or provide secure and convenient access to your residential property, our products are designed to meet your specific needs.

How does it work?

Our automatic doors are ideal for commercial and industrial properties. Our range includes sliding, swing, and revolving doors, all of which are designed to provide ease of access while ensuring your property remains secure. With features such as automatic sensors and remote access control, our doors are perfect for busy commercial properties where time is of the essence.

Our gate systems are perfect for securing your residential property or commercial premises. Our range includes sliding gates, swing gates, and bi-folding gates, all designed to provide maximum security without compromising style. With our automatic gate systems, you can control who enters your property and provide access to authorized personnel only.

Our systems are ideal for controlling the flow of people into public spaces such as stadiums, museums, and airports. Our range includes full-height turnstiles, waist-high turnstiles, and tripod turnstiles, all of which are designed to provide a high level of security while ensuring a smooth flow of people.

Our systems are perfect for commercial and industrial properties where vehicle access needs to be controlled. Our range includes boom barriers, sliding gates, and bollards, all of which are designed to provide maximum security while ensuring that only authorized vehicles are allowed onto your property.

We are committed to providing our customers with the highest level of service and support. Our team of expert technicians is on hand to help you choose the right system for your needs and provide ongoing support and maintenance to ensure that your system continues to operate smoothly.
