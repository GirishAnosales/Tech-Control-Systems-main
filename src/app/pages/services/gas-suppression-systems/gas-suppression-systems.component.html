Gas Suppression Systems

Intro

Gas Suppression Systems are an essential element of any fire protection plan for commercial, industrial, or institutional buildings. They are designed to quickly suppress fires by reducing the oxygen concentration in the protected area, thereby extinguishing the fire without causing damage to the property or equipment.

How does it work?
